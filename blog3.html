A Divine Moment

Image: Golden Crown

Parvati river was just around the corner from hotel so we decided to go there in the evening. I was sitting on a rock beside the river, lost in the moment, entranced by the rush of gushing water when my friend spotted a mountain bathed in the golden light of the setting sun. I felt immensely fortunate to witness such a divine moment. I breathed in the entire golden scenery, soaked in the fragrance of the flowing water, and watched the alluring melody unfold infront of me. I was in a trance.

As the sun slowly moved towards the horizon, the golden light shifted upward, forming a triangle as it neared the mountain's peak. It seemed as if Goddess Parvati had adorned herself with a golden crown.✨
